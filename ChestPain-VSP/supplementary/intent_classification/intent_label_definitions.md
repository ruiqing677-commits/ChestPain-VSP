# Intent Label Definitions

| Intent category | Definition |
|---|---|
| Pain features | Questions about chest pain onset, duration, location, character, severity, radiation, or aggravating/relieving factors. |
| Red flags | Questions about safety-critical symptoms or signs, such as dyspnea, syncope, diaphoresis, neurological deficits, hypotension, tearing pain, or severe acute onset. |
| Risk/history | Questions about cardiovascular risk factors, past medical history, medication use, smoking, family history, thrombotic risk, or relevant comorbidities. |
| Benign / alternative clues | Questions about reflux, musculoskeletal pain, anxiety, rash, positional pain, pleuritic pain, or other lower-risk differential clues. |
| Evidence requests | Requests for physical examination, ECG, troponin, D-dimer, CTA/CTPA, chest X-ray, echocardiography, oxygen saturation, or other diagnostic evidence. |
| Reasoning / disposition | Diagnostic reasoning, differential diagnosis, risk assessment, management plan, admission decision, discharge decision, or referral decision. |
| Other / unclear | Greetings, irrelevant questions, ambiguous utterances, unsupported requests, or utterances that do not clearly match the above categories. |
